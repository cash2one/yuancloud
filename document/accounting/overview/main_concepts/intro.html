    成绩单

    YuanCloud is beautiful accounting software designed for the needs of the 21st century.

    YuanCloud connects directly to your bank or paypal account. Transactions are synchronized every hour and reconciliation is blazing fast. It's like magic.

    Instantly create invoices and send them with just a click. No need to print them.

    YuanCloud可以通过电子邮件或者一般邮件把他们发送给你。

    你的客户在线支付，也为这你立马就可以得到钱。

    YuanCloud accounting is connected with all YuanCloud our apps such as sale, purchase, inventory and subscriptions.

    This way, recording vendor bills is also super quick. Set a vendor, select the purchase order and YuanCloud fills in everything for you automatically.

    Then, just use the SEPA protocol or print checks to pay vendors in batches.

    在YuanCloud中就是这么简单。

    Wait, there is more. You will love the YuanCloud reports. From legal statements to executive summaries, they are fast and dynamic. Use YuanCloud's business intelligence feature to navigate through all your companies data.

    Of course, YuanCloud is mobile too. You can use it to check your accounts on the go.

    现在就开始使用YuanCloud吧，加入我们的2百万用户中。

    概述

    A MercuryPay account (see MercuryPay website.) is required to accept credit card payments in YuanCloud 9 POS with an integrated card reader. MercuryPay only operates with US and Canadian banks making this procedure only suitable for North American businesses. An alternative to an integrated card reader is to work with a standalone card reader, copy the transaction total from the YuanCloud POS screen into the card reader, and record the transaction in YuanCloud POS.

    模块安装

    Go to Apps and install the Mercury Payment Services application.

    Mercury配置

    In the Point of Sale application, click on Configuration ‣ Mercury Configurations and then on Create.

    Introduce your credentials and then save them.

    Then go to Configuration ‣ Payment methods and click on Create. Under the Point of Sale tab you can set a Mercury configuration to the Payment method.

    Finally, go to Configuration ‣ Point of Sale and add a new payment method on the point of sale by editing it.

    Then select the payment method corresponding to your mercury configuration.

    保存更改。

    登记销售

    On the dashboard, you can see your point(s) of sales, click on New Session:

    你会看到销售点主界面：

    On the right you can see the list of your products with the categories on the top. If you click on a product, it will be added in the cart. You can directly set the correct quantity or weight by typing it on the keyboard.

    信用卡付款

    Once the order is completed, click on Payment. You can choose the credit card Payment Method.

    Type in the Amount to be paid with the credit card. Now you can swipe the card and validate the payment.

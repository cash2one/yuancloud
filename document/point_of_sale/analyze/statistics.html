    Point of Sale statistics

    On your dashboard, click on the More button on the point of sale you want to analyse. Then click on Orders.

    You will get the figures for this particular point of sale.

    Global statistics

    Go to Reports ‣ Orders.

    You will get the figures of all orders for all point of sales.

    Cashier statistics

    Go to Reports ‣ Sales Details.

    Choose the dates. Select the cashiers by clicking on Add an item. Then click on Print Report.
